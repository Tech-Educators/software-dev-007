How it works?!

Here is the structure of every searchParam:

Starts with "?"

Then a key such as "name"

Then "="

Then a value, such as "Tim"

So you end up with "?name=Tim"

If you want extra params, separate the key=value pairs with an &

So: "?name=Tim&moustache=true"
